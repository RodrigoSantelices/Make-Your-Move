Make Your Move

Get Organized and Get Moving!

Tired of losing track of your things every time you move?

With Make Your Move you can organize everything you are packing and unpacking to make sure it all arrives to your new destination.

Make a Move List to keep an itinireray of all the objects you are taking, their value, where they can be found, and whether they have been packed or unpacked.

Need to get rid of some things before you move?

Make a Sell List to organize all of the things you plan to sell with the $ you think you'll get for them.

We keep a total of the $ your things are worth!

Want new stuff for your new place?

Stay focused by listing out the things you want, their cost, and where to buy them.

Use the Buy List to add your wishlist items as well as those you've already purchased.

We sum up the cost and subtract it from your budget + sales!

Keep a record of your Moving expenses?

Give yourself a budget to work with.

We keep a balance of your Budget + Sales - Costs to let you know how much $ you need or have left over!
